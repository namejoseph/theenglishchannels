ABOUT

The English Channels is a live music project based in Austin, TX, performing iconic songs from The Cure, Joy Division, New Order, Interpol, Echo & the Bunnymen, and more.

The project features two distinct tribute acts: The Cure Tribute, focused exclusively on the music of The Cure, and You've Lost Control, performing classics from Joy Division, New Order, Interpol, Echo & the Bunnymen, and similar artists.

Members of The English Channels have previously performed with Dream 2 Dream, Joe with the Notes, Easy Compadre, Hamerzya, and Rescue Mission.
