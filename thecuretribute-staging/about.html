About

The English Channels Cure Tribute is an Austin, TX-based band dedicated to celebrating the iconic music of The Cure and Robert Smith. Performing songs from classic albums like Disintegration, Pornography, and The Head on the Door, the band brings to life the sound and atmosphere of The Cure's most beloved eras.

The band has performed at venues across Texas, with dates in Austin, Dallas, San Antonio, and Houston, at venues such as 3TEN ACL Live, The 13th Floor, House of Rock, Elysium, Far Out Lounge, and more. Check the SHOWS page for upcoming dates and future tours.

For all booking inquiries, please visit our CONTACT page or email us at booking@theenglishchannels.com.
