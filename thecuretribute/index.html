About Us

The Cure Tribute is an Austin, TX-based band dedicated to celebrating the iconic music of The Cure and Robert Smith. Performing songs from classic albums like Disintegration, Pornography, and The Head on the Door, the band brings to life the sound and atmosphere of The Cure’s most beloved eras.
